Quest Payments

An incentive-based payment mechanism that drives event attendance through action-rewarded discounts.

How It Works

1

Purchase

Buyer selects an event ticket. A credit card authorization or stablecoin payment is initiated for the full price.

2

Quest

The buyer receives a set of defined incentive actions, each tied to a specific discount off the ticket price.

3

Verify

Each action is provable and verifiable. The system validates completions and applies corresponding discounts.

4

Settle

The final charge is calculated after all eligible incentives resolve. Only the net amount is captured.

Example Incentives

Share the event on social media -5%
Refer a friend who also buys -10%
Check in on time -5%
Attend a sponsor session -3%
Submit post-event feedback -2%
All incentives completed -25%

Payment Architecture

Frontend

Credit Card Payments

Standard card processing via Stripe or similar for a familiar checkout experience. Users never need to interact with crypto.

Backend

x402 + USDC Settlement

All settlement flows through the x402 protocol using USDC on L2 networks. Sub-cent transaction costs, zero protocol fees.

Settlement Strategies

Auth-then-Capture

Hold full amount via credit card authorization, then capture the net amount after incentives are resolved.

Constraint: card network auths expire in 2-7 days. Best for same-day or short-window incentive programs.

Charge-then-Refund

Charge the full ticket price immediately. Issue partial refunds as incentive actions are completed.

Tradeoff: original processing fees (~2.9% + $0.30) are non-recoverable on refunded amounts.

Stablecoin Escrow

Full amount held in a USDC smart contract. Released to the event organizer net of earned discounts.

Most cost-efficient path. Requires crypto-native users or a fiat on-ramp integration.
